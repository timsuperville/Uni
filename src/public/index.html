What is Uni-Manager?

Uni-Manager is an all-in-one platform for managing businesses, churches, and personal tasks. Whether you're handling team schedules, tracking finances, or planning events, Uni-Manager keeps everything organized in one place.

Key Features

  • Church Management: Organize teams, schedules, and worship planning.
  • Business Management: Track inventory, orders, and finances.
  • Personal Management: Manage household tasks, budgets, and schedules.
  • Notifications & Reminders: Stay on top of important events.
  • Multi-Organization Support: Use Uni-Manager for both business and personal life.

Why Choose Uni-Manager?

  • ✅ All-in-One Solution – Manage multiple areas of life in one eco system.
  • ✅ User-Friendly – Simple, clean, and easy to navigate.
  • ✅ Accessible Anywhere – Works across desktop and mobile.
